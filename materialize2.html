    • About Us

    Elise's Pumpkin Loaf Bakery is committed to being an active part of the Oxford community as a family-owned small business. We have curated a unique vibe of relaxation and coziness, inviting customers to spend time in our comfy seating areas.

    While our specialty is pumpkin loaves, our menu diversifies with lattes, espressos, coffee cakes, cupcakes, sourdough loaves, and tarts. Experience the Elise's Pumpkin Loaves difference.
