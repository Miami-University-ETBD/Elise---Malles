Welcome to Elise's Pumpkin Loaf Bakery

What we strive for as a company

Elise's Pumpkin Loaf Bakery is committed to being an active part of the Oxford community as one of the many small business owners, which we like to consider a family. We have specially curated a unique vibe of relaxation and coziness. There is too much hustle and bustle in everyday life to not have a spot for just rest, which is why we invite every customer to spend some time in our comfy seating areas, lovesacks included!

We also believe that our menu, while mainly autumnal, encapsulates the fact that fall flavors can be enjoyed every season. While our specialty is pumpkin loaves, our menu diversifies with lattes, espressos, coffee cakes, cupcakes, sourdough loaves, and tarts. Give us a stop by to experience the Elise's Pumpkin Loaves difference.
